Hello LoveCrumbs

Hello lovecrumbs is one of the cutest cafés you can find in Edinburgh. They are known for their selection of delicious homemade cakes, speciality coffee, grilled cheeses and nice times. It is the perfect place for a studybreak or whenever you want o enjoy a delicious treat. As a plus they are open everyday of the week!

Söderberg Bakery

Söderberg is a Swedish café, the perfect place to drop in to when you are low on energy. It is inspired by the swedish "fika", which means they serve all kinds of buns such as cardamom, cinnamon and regular sugarbuns. If you are feeling a bit hungry, they also serve a variety of food. They have bakerys in 7 different locations in Edinburgh and are open all day.

Union Brew Lab

Union Brew lab is famous for their coffee and that is the heart of what they do. They serve both brunch and lunch together with their coffee, which of course is coming from local producers. They are situated close to the University of Edinburgh, which means it is perfect to go there for a coffee and a snack with your friends in between classes.
